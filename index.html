Oatmeal bowls

A good breakfast is the start of a great day
G-OAT is a revolutionary eatery specializing in oatmeal bowls, smoothies, sweets and more. We provide a healthy, delicious, high-quality and fast meal option while striving to be conscientious of our local community and minimize our earthly impact. When you choose to serve a breakfast made by G-OAT you will leave feeling ready to attack your day with a balanced mind-set. And you will be at ease, knowing that your decision was not only a personally healthful one, but one that cast a vote for a better world at large. And as a bonus, it will have been a whole heap of fun to eat.

G-OAT products follow three simple rules

ORGANIC

Our food is mostly organic. What’s not is at least thoughtfully sourced, often from a small farm or producer that is simply not certified.

NUTRITIOUS

Everything we serve we believe to be good for you. It’s all about eating real food. We boast zero unpronounceable ingredients (as soon as you learn to say “Acai” that is), and dish out truckloads of fruits, veggies, nuts, seeds, and grains.

ENVIRONMENTALLY FRIENDLY

In everything we do, we aim to minimize our earthly impact. The integrity of our plant-based menu has a particularly small footprint, and we strive to minimize our operation’s impact in myriad other ways as well.
